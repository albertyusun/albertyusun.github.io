What I’m doing now

As a Data Plus Research Fellow, I'm expanding my machine learning and NLP toolbox. I’m working with a team of Duke professors and graduate students to investigate the early histories of consumerism. We're using TensorFlow, word2vec, and multiple other packages in Python and R to conduct structural topic modeling and word embeddings on 26,000 old English texts. GitHub. Project Info.

Furthermore, as a Huang Fellow in Duke’s Science and Society Initiative, I’m investigating the intersection between science and law. A few of us have created the Huang Fellows Racial Equity Committee, and we're working to make structural reforms in Duke S&S and invite Black speakers in response to recent acts of anti-Black police brutality.
